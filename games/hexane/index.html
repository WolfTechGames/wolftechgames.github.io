Hexane is an upcoming action-adventure game.

Hexane is an isometric action-adventure game being developed by two guys at Wolftech Games. It puts you in the role of astrochemist Casey Robustelli amidst a rebellion against Earth. Casey must find the man behind the coup, and stop his evil Revolution. Casey uses his immense knowledge of chemistry, and his home-made arsenal of fiery and explosive weapons, to fight the rebels. You will traverse the world, fighting relentless enemies and defeating incredible bosses on their journey to find the mastermind behind the Revolution.

Hexane is isometric and level-based, and each level's terrain will be comprised of hexagons, such as those pictured. Casey can equip two weapons at a time, each with two unique methods of attack. As you go further into the rich and wondrous worlds, the enemies will get tougher and tougher. However, you will also gain more weapons and experience on your journey.

You will become a legend, a saviour of mankind.
Are you ready to step up to the challenge?
