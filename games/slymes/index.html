Grow big enough to engulf the world in Slymes!

Slymes is a multiplayer arena cell-game, inspired by games like agar.io, Osmos, and slither.io. Unlike those games however, Slymes puts you in a vast, three-dimensional world. As a slime, you must absorb small blobs to grow big and strong, giving you the power to trample your insignificant opponents. You do lose a bit of speed, but you make up for it with the ability to eject excess mass. You can even shoot parts of yourself to knock environmental hazards to your enemies.


You can play in the Classic mode, where you simply have to grow the biggest and climb the server leaderboards. If that doesn't suit you, worry not! Slymes comes with many other, different game modes. Work as a team to acheive victory over the enemy in Team Deathmatch, or infiltrate the danger zone and recover precious artifacts in Capture the Flag. You can also deck out your Slyme with various unlockable skins, hats, and trails, which you earn just by proving your worth in the battlefield and gaining experience in blob-to-blob combat.
