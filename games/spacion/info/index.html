Info

Spacion is a mutliplayer survival game that takes place in a vast procedurally generated solar system full of various planets that will force you to face harsh conditions and to fight for your survival.

Gameplay

Your journey begins on a randomly generated planet.

You may:

  • gather resources.
  • craft a shelter to protect you from threats such as players.
  • engineer your own space craft that can transport you to other planets.
  • group up with friends and form a squadron for space battle.
  • go solo and assassinate players for their loot.

    • Modding

    Spacion has full modding capability, this will allow users to expand the game by
    adding new items, ship parts, building pieces and custom functionality.
